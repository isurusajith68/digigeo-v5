Help File

This interactive mapping interface enables companies to highlight their global properties in relation to other zones, mines, deposits and properties.

Highlighted properties are coloured and additional property information is provided.

Users can select filters and searches to explore areas of exploration properties and the companies that are exploring for commodities globally.

Assets, properties, and claim layers are provided to show the results of the searches in context.

Properties are created by compiling a group of claims. Assets fit within a property and there can be multiple assets within one property.

Filters

Companies and properties can be searched using the 3 Filters.

Exploration Areas: Search for specific Exploration Areas within North America such as Athabasca Basin, Golden Triangle, James Bay. Data has been tagged to these for easier research.

Properties: Search for any property by Name, Country, State/Province, Mining Area, Asset Type, Commodity

Companies: Search for any Company by name or stock code.

Map Layers

Map Layers are divided into 3 categories; Assets, Properties and Claims Sub-layers within can be turned off or on depending on how much detail is required. Different layers turn on/off at various zoom levels. Layers can be turned off/on by selecting the icon.  

Assets

Operating Mine: Mine that is currently operating or may be Suspended or on Care and Maintenance

Historical Mine: Previously operating mine but now closed; or could be being re-evaluated.

Deposits: Asset with a compliant resource or with a historical resource

Zone: Used to depict a property that has had at last one drill hole; the zone may or may not be spatially located

Occurrences: Used to depict a property that has not had any drilling but may have any other type of exploration or surveys.

Properties

Property Points: A point used to locate a property on the map. It is usually associated with an asset.

Property Outlines: Shows the actual property configuration based on the aggregation of claims.

Claims

Claims: Seamless claims across all provinces and territories in Canada, BLM claims for western United States, Mexico, South America and other countries as available and added to the data base.

Mining Areas: This layer turns on/off the exploration area blue outlines.

Searching

Users can immediately start to search by zooming in on the map. All the properties are clustered in yellow dots with the number of properties within the cluster shown. The clusters separate into more clusters as the zoom continues. Eventually the layers of data appear on the map.

 

Exploration Areas

To search Exploration Areas, click on the box and select the filter icon

A menu box will open.

Searching can be done by selecting an area within the list and clicking on the blue arrow on the right or selecting a country from the country list.

To date only Exploration areas within Canada, United States and Mexico are available. To search for all companies and properties globally, use the Properties or Companies Filter.

The map will zoom to that area and show the featured properties. A box will open to the right of the legend with a list showing the companies with featured properties. Select a company with the  icon and a new box with information on the company and a list of their properties in that area.

For information on the property, select the icon

To zoom to the property, select the icon

 The All Companies list under the Featured Companies, is a list of all companies and their properties in that area, including the Featured Companies.

Selecting a company will show their properties.

Navigating is the same as for the Featured Companies.

Another search can be done by selecting the icon at the bottom of the menu.

Properties

Global Properties can be searched by selecting the  box and the blue filter icon.

Search for a property by name or select from the various menus. The selected properties are shown in the list on the right.

If more than one commodity is chosen, the search will identify all properties with either or both.

Select to display the selected properties on the map.

Companies

All companies can be searched by selecting the  box and the blue filter icon. A menu box will open.

Companies can be searched by stock code or name. Select the  icon and a list of the company’s and a new box will show the company with a link to its profile and featured and all properties. Selecting a property will zoom to it on the map.

To search for another company, close the box with the list of properties by selecting the  at the top right-hand corner of the box.

Cumulative Searches

Three consecutive searches can be made with each of the three filters. A user can search Exploration Area, find a Property, or a Company. All three searches remain active until the user changes them or closes the website.

All three searches can be closed by choosing the  icon at bottom of the legend on the left-hand side.

Map Controls

Map controls work like any Google map.

Zooming in and out can is controlled by a mouse wheel or be selecting the + or – icons.

Selecting the globe icon returns to the overall map view.

Selecting the left facing arrow, closes the legend. Selecting the right facing arrow opens the legend.

Map Location

The bottom right-hand corner of the map shows the current zoom scale and the latitude and longitude of a point on the map.
